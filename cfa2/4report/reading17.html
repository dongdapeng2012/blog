Pensions and Other Post-Employment Benefit (OPB)

--------------------

  • pension obligation

    • IFRS: PVDBO present value of the define benefit obligation

    • GAAP: PBO projected benefit obligation

    • GAAP also identify 2 pension liability

      • VBO vested benefit obligation

      • ABO accumulated benefit obligation (vested and non-vested)

      • PBO > ABO > VBO

  • DC 的财报处理

    • 作为expense计入IS表

    • 较少的DC pension相关的应付负债 accrual liability 计入BS表

    • 尚未支付的供款计入应付流动负债 accrual current liability

  • DB 的财报处理

    • BS表

      • fund status = fair value of plan assets - PVDBO



    • IS表: 周期性养老金成本

      • periodic pension cost 计入IS的P&L或OCI

      • total periodic pension cost disclose in the notes

      • periodic pension cost 分为三部分

        • service cost, result of EE's service

          • IFRS下, 作为expense计入P&L

          • GAAP下

            • current service cost 在P&L中确认

            • past service cost, because of changes to the plan, 在OCI中确认, 在P&L中摊销

        • net interest expense/income

          • IFRS下 net interest expense/income = net pension liablility or asset × interest rate, 计入P&L

          • GAAP下

            • interest expense on pension obligation, is based on liability, 在P&L中确认

            • expected return on pension assets = plan assets × expected return, 在P&L中确认

        • remeasurement

          • actuarial G&L = changes in a company's pension obligation arising from changes in actuarial assumptions

          • IFRS下, 计入OCI, 后续报告期不在P&L中摊销

            • net return on plan assets = actual return - (plan assets × interest rate)

          • GAAP下, 精算 actuarial G&L including differences between the actual and expected returns on plan assets

            • 立即计入P&L或OCI, 后续用corridor(10%阈值判断是否需要摊销)或faster method摊销计入P&L

            • differences (differences - expected) = actual return - (plan assets × expected return)

        • 未资本化的部分

          • IFRS不区分P&L或OCI

          • GAAP要求计入P&L, 要求按net amount记入same line itme

    • IFRS 用 projected unit credit method 计量 DB obligation

    • 在IFRS和GAAP下, assumed rate of increase in compensation, discount rate 对结果的影响很大

    • GAAP下, P&L 中 expected return on plan assets 会减少 periodic pension costs

    • impact of key DB pension assumptions on BS and IS

      • 高折现率 --> BS - lower obligation; IS - lower periodic pension cost

      • 高增长率 --> BS - higher obligation; IS - higer service cost

      • 高盈利期望 --> BS - 无影响, BS用fair value of plan assets; IS - IFRS 无影响, GAAP lower periodic pension expense

  • 不同会计准则下的调整GAAP --> IFRS

    • BS/IS 中扣除past service cost

    • BS/IS 中将按预期收益估计的回报改为按折现率估计的回报

  • CF - 企业缴纳的提存金(供款) vs. 当期pension cost

    • 供款盈余 overcontribution --> CFO 增加; CFF 减少

    • 供款赤字 undercontribution --> CFO 减少; CFF 增加

Share-based Compensation

--------------------

  • IFRS和GAAP要求年报披露key elements of management compensation

  • 会计

    • no cash outlay

    • 计入expense, 拉低earnings

    • 在IFRS和GAAP下都要求基于fair value披露

      • the nature and extent of share-based compensation arrangement

      • fair value

      • effect on the company's income and financial position

  • advangate

    • no cash outlay

  • disadvantage

    • 经济环境不好的时候, 管理层的影响很有限

    • 管理层可能为达到股价目标而故意激进或保守

    • 稀释股东股权

  • equity-settled

    • stock grant

    • stock options

  • cash-settled

    • stock appreciation rights SAR

      • 有限的下行风险和无限的上行获利

      • 不稀释股东股份

      • 当期 cash outflow

    • phantom stock - based on hypothetical stock
