STANDARD VII: RESPONSIBILITIES AS A CFA INSTITUTE MEMBER OR CFA CANDIDATE

--------------------

Standard VII(A) Conduct as Participants in CFA Institute Programs

Highlights

  • Confidential Program Information
    • 禁止透露具体问题和考试范围

  • Additional CFA Program Restrictions
    • 禁止透露具体问题和考试范围
    • deliberation related to the exam process

  • Expressing an Opinion
    • 可以同意或不同意CFA观点
    • 但禁止透露具体问题和考试范围

Examples

Example 8 (Compromising CFA Institute Integrity as a Volunteer)

Andy 是几家小公司的顾问, 同时也是所在城市的chair for the CFA Institute society. Andy schedules only his clients to make presentations to the society and excludes other companies.

Standard VII(B) Reference to CFA Institute, the CFA Designation, and the CFA Program

Highlights

  • CFA Institute Membership
    • 每年提交 Professional Conduct Statement
    • 年缴会费

  • CFA Designation
      Proper References
    • CFA提高技能
    • CFA帮助未来职业发展
    • Andy 仅用三年就通过了所有CFA考试
    • CFA是受国际认可的投资行业综合学习项目的证书
    • CFA是业界最高证书之一highest set of credentials
    • 作为CFA, Andy is commited to 最高道德标准

      Improper References
    • CFA 持证人有更好的业绩
    • CFA 持证人是最合格的投资经理
    • CFA 持证人在投资中创造最多价值
    • CFA 持证人保证能更准确估值
    • Andy 仅用三年就通过了所有CFA考试, 他是精英
    • Andy CFA, Level II
    • Andy CFA, Expected 2011
    • Andy, Charter Pending

  • Proper Usage of the CFA Marks
      Correct
    • He is one of two CFA charterholders in the company
    • He earned the right to use the Chartered Financial Analyst designation
    • Andy Li, CFA
    • Andy Li, Chartered Financial Analyst
    • Level I candidate in the CFA Program
    • Passed Level I of the CFA examination in 2018
    • Andy 通过CFA三级考试, and may be eligible for the CFA charter 一旦满足工作经验要求
    • CFA Charter, 2009, CFA Institute (optional: Charlottesville, Virginia, USA)

      Incorrect
    • He is one of two CFAs in the company
    • He is a Chartered Financial Analyst
    • Andy, C.F.A
    • Andy, cfa
    • Andy, a CFA-type portfolio manager
    • The focus is on Chartered Financial Analysis
    • CFA-equivalent program
    • Swiss-CFA
    • Andy Chartered Financial Analysts, Inc
    • Smith, CFA CFA 不许比名字更显眼
    • Chartered Financial Analyst (CFA), September 2018
    • CFA Level I. CFA degree expected in 2020
    • CFA (Passed Finalist)
    • CFA Charter Pending
    • Pending CFA Charterholder
    • CFA Charter, 2009, CFA Society of the UK
    • 超级熊 CFA (Online social media user name)

Examples

Example 1 (Passing Exams in Consecutive Years)

AAA公司广告说全体分析师都是CFA持证人并且都一次通过考试, 广告还连接到AAA公司业绩行业领先

Comment : 不能将CFA和业绩好联系起来，不能将一次过和业绩好联系起来


Example 2 (Right to Use CFA Designation)

Andy Li, CFA, 有一段时间没有提交PCS也没有交会费, 后来继续提交PCS并交了会费, 之后在名片中写Andy, CFA

Example 3 omit

Example 4 (CFA Logo—Individual Use Only)

AAA 公司 + CFA logo

Comment : CFA仅用于个人, 不用于企业

Example 5 omit

Example 6 (Order of Professional and Academic Designations)

Andy Li, CFA, PhD 或 Andy, Phd, CFA

Comment : 多个称号顺序无关, 必须全名
