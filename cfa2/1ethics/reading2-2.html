Standard II(A) Material Nonpublic Information

Highlights

  • Social Media

    As long as the information reaches all clients or is open to the public, these platforms would be public.

    M&C, as required by Standard I(A), should also complete all appropriate regulatory filings distributed through social media platforms.


  • Using Industry Experts

    可以为外部专家支付报酬

    不能基于外部专家的内幕有行动


  • Investment Research Reports

    知名分析师的结论是重要公开消息

    公众可得仍然可能是不公开消息, 因为分析师的研究过程不是公开的, 研究资源可能不是公开的

适用情景

  • Mosaic 理论
  • 社交媒体
  • 聘用行业专家
  • 研究报告 看是否公开发表, 知名投资人的投资行为视为公开

    Recommended Procedures for Compliance
  • Achieve Public Dissemination
    • if nonpublic, 尽可能促使公开
    • If cannot public, 只对相关主管和合规人员分享, 禁止基于此信息做出投资行为过改变推荐等级
    • must not knowingly engage in privately disclose material nonpublic information

  • Issue Press Releases
    • Issue Press Releases prior to analyst meetings and calls and scripting those meetings and calls
    • If material nonpublic information is disclosed for the first time, the company should promptly issue a press release or make the information public.

  • Firewall Elements
    • control of relevant interdepartmental communications
    • review of EE trading through watch lists, restricted lists, and rumor lists
    • 当公司有内幕消息的时候, 加强交易监管
    • 交易, 交流procedure, 禁止跨部门兼任任职

Example 2 (Controlling Nonpublic Information)

Example 5 (“Pump- Priming” Strategy)

FFF attempts to demonstrate the best liquidity. It let members to commit to a substantial minimum trading volume on the new future over a specific period in exchange for substantial reductions of regular commissions.

Comment : Attempts to mislead participants about the actual liquidity

Comment : In posting the false information, Andy misleads others into believing the companies were likely to be acquired.
