STANDARD IV: DUTIES TO EMPLOYERS

--------------------

Standard IV(A) Loyalty

适用情景

  • 放弃与雇主正当利益冲突的行为
  • 离职
  • 社交媒体
  • 告发
  • 考虑雇佣关系本质 雇主支出的资源, 员工是否脚踩两条船

Examples

Example 1-2 omit

Example 3 (Addressing Rumors)

Their internal inquiries are met with denials from PPP management, but the rumors persist. Feeling left in the dark, Andy contemplates leading an employee buyout of PPP's equity management business.

Comment : An employee-led buyout of business is consistent with Standard IV(A) because it would rest on the permission of the employer and the clients. In this case, employees suspect the senior managers, Andy should consult legal counsel.

Example 4-5 omit

Example 6 (Soliciting Former Clients)

When Andy begins working for EEE, he wants to contact his former clients because he knows them well and is certain that many will follow him to his new employer.

Comment :

    出现以下情形时视为违反S&C:
  • client records are the property of the firm
  • noncompete agreement that covers contact with former clients


Example 7 (Starting a New Firm)

Without notice to his firm, Andy registers with government authorities to start an company competing with his firm, but he does not actively seek clients.

Comment : Andy's preparation for his own firm is ok if the preparations is done on his own time outside the office and if Andy will not be soliciting clients or not operating the new company.

Example 8 omit

Example 9 (Externally Compensated Assignments)

Andy is a research analyst with TTT Investments. He works part time as a mayor for his hometown, receiving compensation.

Comment : Andy should discuss with his employer and get a agreement.

Example 10-13 omit

Example 14 (Leaving an Employer)

Andy was informed that RRR would contact clients immediately about his departure and introduce his replacement. Realizing that his LinkedIn network would be a valuable resource, Andy updated his profile several days following his departure from RRR. Andy still contact with the clients as friends in LinkedIn. Andy has signed a non-solicitation agreement that requires him to wait two years before soliciting RSI clients for any investment- related services.

Comment : 除非有竞业协议禁止任何与客户的交流，否则在LinkedIn上的处理不会违规.


Example 15 (Confidential Firm Information)

When Andy disagrees with the opinions of CCC, he uses his personal blog to express his own opinions . He believes this provides clients a more complete view.
